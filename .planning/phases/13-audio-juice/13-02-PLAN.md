**AnimatingDie.tsx (97 lines):**
- Used for lock animations (human + AI), AI unlock animations
- Has `onComplete()` callback — fires when animation reaches 100%
- 0.6s default lerp duration, ease-in-out cubic + parabolic arc
- Scene tracks completed count, clears when all done

**MitosisDie.tsx (182 lines):**
- Unlock/mitosis animation — 3 phases:
  - LERP (0–0.5s): fly from locked slot to pool area
  - SHAKE (0.5–1.3s): vibrate at target, amplitude ramps 0.02→0.12
  - SPLIT (1.3–1.7s): single die becomes two, ease out to targets
- Has `onComplete()` callback

**SpawningDie / ExitingDie in DicePool.tsx:**
- SpawningDie: fly from avatar position, scale overshoot 0→1.15→1, tumble during flight
- ExitingDie: pop (1→1.3 ease-out) + shrink (1.3→0 ease-in), 0.45s total

**Lock animation stagger:** Each lock has a `delay` prop (0, 0.25, 0.5...) for sequential locking.
