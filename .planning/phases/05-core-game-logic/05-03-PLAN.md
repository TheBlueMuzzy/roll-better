**Store — scoreRound():**
- For each player: if lockedDice.length === 8 (winner), calculate score:
  `score = Math.max(0, 8 - Math.max(0, (poolSize - 8) * 2))`
  (poolSize here is the TOTAL dice the player accumulated, including the 8 locked ones that are now "used")
  Wait — re-read rules. Scoring: "8 points max. -2 points for each die in your pool beyond 8."
  The "pool" at scoring time = the dice that were in the player's rolling pool when they won. Since all 8 are locked, the pool is what's LEFT over. Pool = poolSize (remaining unlocked dice).
  Actually, "pool" in the rules means total dice the player has/had. Let me re-read:
  "Pool of 8 → 8 pts (perfect)" — this means if you had exactly 8 dice total (all locked, none remaining), perfect score.
  "Pool of 9 → 6 pts" — 9 total dice, 8 locked + 1 remaining in pool.
  So: totalDice = lockedDice.length + poolSize = 8 + remaining. Score = 8 - max(0, (totalDice - 8) * 2).
  Simplified: score = max(0, 8 - poolSize * 2) where poolSize = remaining unlocked dice when you win.
  Even simpler: score = max(0, 8 - (totalDice - 8) * 2) where totalDice = 8 + poolSize at win time.
- Add score to player.score
- Mark which players won this round (for handicap)

**Store — applyHandicap():**
- For each player:
  - If they won this round: `startingDice = Math.max(1, startingDice - 1)`
