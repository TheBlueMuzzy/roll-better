    4. Confirm: "Reclaiming your seat..." UI appears (auto-matched via persistentId)
    5. Confirm: Player automatically claims their OLD seat (the bot that replaced them)
    6. Wait for phase boundary
    7. Confirm: Player re-enters the game in their original seat position
