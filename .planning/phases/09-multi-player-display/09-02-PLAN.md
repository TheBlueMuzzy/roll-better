<task type="auto">
  <name>Task 2: Rework goal enter — emerge from Star icon with scale + roll</name>
  <files>src/components/GoalRow.tsx, src/components/Scene.tsx</files>
  <action>
Replace the current left-slide-in enter animation with a star-origin emergence:

**Enter behavior:**
- Each die starts at the Star icon position (GoalProfileGroup's world X, same Z as goal row).
- Initial scale = 0.
- Each die lerps from star position → its final slot position (getSlotX(i)).
- During flight, scale interpolates 0 → 1 (linear or slight overshoot).
- During flight, apply continuous rotation (tumbling) — rotate around X and Z axes to simulate rolling. Use a steady angular velocity (not physics — just visual). Something like: rotX += delta * 8, rotZ += delta * 6 (radians/sec).
- At arrival, snap to the correct face rotation via getRotationForFace(value).
- Stagger: ~30-50ms per die (left to right).
- Duration per die: ~0.5s.
- Easing on position: easeOut (decelerating — dice slow as they reach their slot).

**Star position:** GoalRow needs to know the Star icon's X position. Either:
- Pass it as a prop from Scene (starX prop).
- Or compute it: leftmost getSlotX(0) minus the same offset used for profile groups.
- Use the latter to keep it self-contained.

**Rotation on arrival:** After the lerp completes (t >= 1), set rotation to getRotationForFace(value) so the correct number faces up. During flight, the tumble rotation is purely visual.
