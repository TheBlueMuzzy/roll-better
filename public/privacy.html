Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026

No Data Collection

Roll Better collects no personal data. None. Zero.

No Cookies, No Analytics, No Tracking

This game does not use cookies, analytics services, or any form of tracking. There are no third-party services that collect data.

No Account Required

You never need to create an account. Room codes are temporary, randomly generated, and not stored anywhere after the game session ends.

Browser Memory Only

Game state exists only in your browser's memory during play. A client ID is stored in sessionStorage solely to maintain your connection during a game session — it is automatically cleared when you close the tab.

Ephemeral Connections

Online multiplayer uses PartyKit WebSocket connections that are entirely ephemeral. There is no server-side logging of player activity, game history, or connection metadata.

No Third-Party Services

Roll Better does not integrate with any third-party services that collect, process, or store user data.

No In-App Purchases

Roll Better is completely free with no in-app purchases, subscriptions, or monetization of any kind.

Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
